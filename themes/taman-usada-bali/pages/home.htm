Welcome to Taman Usada Bali

Taman Usada Bali is a wellness destination purposely designed for seekers to get healed by the ancient healing therapies that the locals still practice nowadays on the island of Bali. The invaluable local wisdom the enlightened beings need to preserve for the global wellness of a healthy lifestyle in the world of disruption.

Where are we?

Nestled in the village of Desa Baru, Banjar Baru, Tabanan Bali, popularly known as the rice barn of Bali Island, where Jati Luwih’s rice terraced paddies, the UNESCO heritage, well-known tourist destination is within a few minute’s drive away. The indigenous people strongly preserve their living traditions based on Balinese Hinduism ways of life. Rituals are an everyday scene. The vibe of spirituality resonates with the daily locals’ living.
